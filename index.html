Adopt

Each year in the United States alone, 2.7 million adoptable dogs and cats are euthanized because not many people adopt from shelters. Because space within shelters is limited, staff members are required to decide which animals will be euthanized. The number of euthanized animals could be reduced dramatically if more people adopted pets instead of buying them

Donate

Private, non-profit organizations rely on donations and grants to fund their programs. Many private agencies are limited-access, sometimes called “no-kill” facilities, because they do not euthanize animals to make room for more animals. Monetary donations go towards food, shelter and medical care. Donations can be given once, or every month.

Volunteer

Volunteers are needed! We have a constant intake of animals. Each one of these animals needs your love and care. Your help will never go unnoticed and you will never feel like you aren’t helping in some way. There will always be a dog to walk, a cat to groom or some animal to give your love to.
